# Show FPS
There are multiple ways to show the FPS in your unreal. 

One is to go to editor preferences and performance and click `Show Frame Rate and Memory`.

![Show FPS In Title Bar Img](/img/Unreal/ShowFPSInTitleBar.png)

Another way is to write `stat fps` into the console command field.
