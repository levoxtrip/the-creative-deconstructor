# 32-bit float Pixel Format

Setting the *pixel format* of a TOP to `32-bit float` allows to have values that are bigger than 1 and smaller than 0. You see in the upper noise the values get cut off when they are outside of the range between 0 and 1. They are not in the 32-bit noise.
