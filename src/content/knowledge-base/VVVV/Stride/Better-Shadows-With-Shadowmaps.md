# Better Shadows With Shadowmaps
To increase the quality of your shadows in your Stride scene you can add light shadow maps. VVVV has different shadow maps for the different lights.
`LightPointShadowMap`
`LightDirectionalShadowMap`
`LightStandardShadowMap`
